Real-time delta tracking.

Taptic doesn't just track time. It calculates active participation deltas—keystrokes, lines of code, and characters added or removed—giving you a true picture of output.

  • Heartbeat Flushes: Lightweight background updates every 30 seconds.
  • Content Injection: Native integration into your browser's DOM for accurate measurement.
  • Secure Tokens: Automated setup links for team members with encrypted authentication.
